Green River Rafting Company

Ever wanted to take a ride on the famously unpilotable green river? Trust us! We've got the skills to show you sights unseen!

History

Founded in 1804 by a business savvy raccoon and skilled rafting possum, the green river rafting company was not an instant success. While the quality of the experience has always been top notch, most people take execption to riding with a raccoon and a possum as their guides and navigators.

In 2008, the two founders descendants, with an influx of cash from shorting the market, built a human replica they could use to pilot passengers down the green river. Since then They have helped dozens of groups and adventurers experience the beauty of the green river
