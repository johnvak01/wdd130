Package 1: Green River Group Trip

Have a Full Group who wants to raft the Green river? Sign up for our Group Package for a discount and priority scheduling. Enjoy your Personalised Group Trip.

Group Trips rent out whole rafts at once. For partial rafts you can either pay for the whole raft, or allow our Package 2 Joiners to ride along in the spare space.

Package 2: A-La-Carte Green River Rafting

Looking to meet new and interesting people? Join as a Ride along as one of Our A-La-Carte Rafters. You'll be paired with a group of complete strangers for an unexpected Journey.

Worried You'll be paired with unsocial weirdos? no worries! Our experienced guides have recieved special training to handle any akward social encounters, and are empowered to act to ensure the whole group has a good time, regardless of background or proclivities.

Package 3: Guided Kayaking

Want to enjoy the wisdom of our guides while not being beholden to them? Sign up for our Guided Kayaking trips.

In a Kayak, our guides can take you down various routes according to your skill level and preferences. From easy currents to thundering rapids, this is the option for those who want the ultimate custom experience.
